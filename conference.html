Girl Genius is a 501(c)(3) aiming to create an inclusive community where people of color in STEAM (non-binary and trans-inclusive) are given a voice to empower the next generation of female leaders.

We enable girls to become change makers in their community by publishing magazine issues by women in STEAM, organizing free virtual conferences and workshops, and connecting girls with women in the workforce.
